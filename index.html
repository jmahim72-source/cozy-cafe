Our Story

Crafted with Love & Heart

The Cozy Hearth began with a simple dream: to create a space that feels like a warm hug. Founded by coffee enthusiast Clara Vance, our cafe is built on the belief that a great cup of coffee has the power to bring people together, spark inspiration, and offer a moment of pure comfort in a busy world.

We roast our coffee in small batches, bake our pastries fresh every single morning, and curate an environment where you can slow down, read a book, or catch up with an old friend.

Locally Sourced

Partnering with nearby organic farms for fresh milk, eggs, and seasonal ingredients.

Handcrafted with Love

Every single pour, shot, and pastry is meticulously prepared by our expert baristas.
